Why we're building this01 / 03
01 The stake

Insurance has never mattered more.

Climate volatility, rising asset values, thinner household buffers. The cost of being under-insured has moved from an inconvenience to a structural risk for Australian households and small businesses. Well-placed cover, delivered at volume, matters more than it ever has.

02 The gap

Brokers are the channel, and they're out of leverage.

Brokers are the ones sitting across the table from real clients. To grow their book without grinding themselves down, they need the tools to move faster on every policy, and deliver the same quality of advice at many times the volume.

03 The tool

AI is the unlock. This market is about to move fast.

Purpose-built AI, wired into the workflow a broker actually runs (new business, renewals, claims), collapses the time per policy and changes the economics of how much a broker can profitably write. The transformation is not theoretical. It's arriving now.
