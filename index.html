Common questions05 / 05

What does Tenzi do?

Tenzi is workflow automation software for Australian general insurance brokers. It takes the manual parts of new business, renewals, and claims off your desk: client details captured once, quote slips and submissions assembled, renewal packs drafted, claims kept moving. You review and place; the platform types, formats, and chases.

Who is Tenzi for?

Australian general insurance brokers of every shape: Authorised Representatives under AFSL networks, brokerages holding their own licence, and the licensee groups behind them. If you run new business, renewals, and claims for real clients, you're who we build for.

What is the design partner program?

A three-month working seat alongside the founding team. Month one maps your workflows and wires Tenzi into the systems you already run, month two you put real client work through the platform, month three we measure the impact in hours and dollars. Your side is about an hour a week. Cohort 01 is full; Cohort 02 opens September 2026 with six spots.

Can I buy Tenzi today?

Not yet. The platform is in active testing with our first design partner cohort. The way in is the design partner program: register interest for Cohort 02 through the contact form below.

Who is behind Tenzi?

Tenzi Pty Ltd, a seed-funded company based in Melbourne, co-founded by Roshan Khozouei, previously General Manager of Data Labs at Australia Post and a Scaling AI Industry think tank leader at CSIRO's National AI Centre. A provisional patent covering form-filling automation has been filed.

What is resources.tenzi.ai?

Our free data arm: ASIC register movement dashboards, AR profile data, and operator runbooks for new business, renewals, and claims. Published free for the industry, no paywall, no sign-up gate.
